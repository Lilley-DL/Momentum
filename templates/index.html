Welcome to Momentum

Your Fitness, Nutrition, and Hydration—All in One Place

Welcome to Momentum, your all-in-one solution for tracking workouts, meals, macros, and water intake. Whether you're a beginner building healthy habits or an experienced athlete optimizing performance, our app is designed to help you stay on track and achieve your goals.

Why Choose Momentum?

  • 📊 Workout Tracking – Log exercises, reps, and sets to monitor progress.
  • 🍽️ Nutrition Tracking – Log meals, and macros to track nutrition.
  • 💧 Water Intake Monitoring – Set daily hydration goals and track intake.
  • 🔗 Seamless Experience – An intuitive interface that makes tracking effortless.

Our Mission

We believe tracking your fitness and nutrition shouldn’t feel like a chore. Our goal is to make it simple, effective, and rewarding for everyone. Whether you're aiming to build muscle, lose weight, or maintain a healthy lifestyle, Momentum is here to support you every step of the way.

Monetization & Support

To keep the app free and accessible, we include carefully selected affiliate links to fitness-related products and services. If you choose to purchase through these links, you’ll be supporting the app at no extra cost to you. We also use non-intrusive ads to sustain development and improve the app over time.
